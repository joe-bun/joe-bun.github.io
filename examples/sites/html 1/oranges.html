Oranges

Description

Oranges are a good source of vitamin C. They originated possibly in Southeast Asia, and were cultivated in China as early as 2500 BC. Orange trees are grown in tropical and subtropical climates.

Varieties of Oranges

There are a few common varieties of oranges, from the following regions:

  • Florida
    • Homosassa
    • Lue Gim Gong
    • Midsweet
  • India
    • Pineapple
    • Sathgudi
  • Italy
    • Calabrese
    • Maltese
    • Moro
    • Tarocco
  • Morocco
    • Cadanera
    • Vema
    • Vicieda
